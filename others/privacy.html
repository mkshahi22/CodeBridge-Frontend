Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 28, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how CodeBridge collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal information. By using our platform, you consent to the practices described in this document. We are committed to transparency and protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide, improve, and secure CodeBridge services. This includes:

•
Account Information: Name, email address, password (encrypted), profile picture
•
Generation Data: Natural language prompts, uploaded files, AI-generated code outputs
•
Usage Data: Browser type, device information, IP address, pages visited, timestamps
•
Analytics: Feature usage patterns, error logs, performance metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

✓ Authenticate and manage your account securely
✓ Generate AI-powered code through Kiro integration
✓ Personalize your experience and improve platform features
✓ Send security alerts, important updates, and notifications
✓ Prevent fraud, abuse, and maintain platform safety
✓ Analyze usage patterns to enhance performance and user experience
✓ Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

3. Data Storage & Protection

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and modern backend security practices:

• Passwords are encrypted using secure hashing algorithms

• JWT tokens for secure authentication

• HTTPS/TLS encryption for data transmission

• Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments

• Access controls and monitoring for database security

While we implement rigorous security measures, no system is completely immune to risks. We recommend users follow security best practices like using strong passwords and enabling two-factor authentication.

4. Sharing of Information

CodeBridge does NOT sell or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share limited data with:

→
Authentication Services: Google, GitHub, LinkedIn for social login
→
AI Service Providers: Kiro AI for code generation (prompts and files)
→
Analytics & Hosting: Service providers for performance monitoring and infrastructure
→
Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or government request

5. AI Usage & Privacy

Prompts and files submitted for code generation are processed securely through Kiro AI. Generated code may include patterns learned from publicly available sources, documentation, and open-source repositories.

⚠️ Important: Review all AI-generated code carefully before using it in production environments. Ensure compliance with licensing requirements and security standards.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

CodeBridge uses minimal cookies and similar technologies for:

• Session management and authentication (JWT tokens stored in localStorage)

• Remembering user preferences and settings

• Understanding usage patterns and improving functionality

• Analytics to enhance platform performance

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect platform functionality.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

✓ Access Your Data

Request a copy of all personal information we hold about you

✓ Correct Information

Update or correct inaccurate personal data

✓ Delete Your Account

Request permanent deletion of your account and data

✓ Export Your Data

Download your code history and prompts

✓ Opt-Out of Communications

Disable non-essential email notifications

✓ Data Portability

Transfer your data to another service

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@codebridge.ai

8. Third-Party Links & Services

CodeBridge may contain links to external websites, services, or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of third-party services. Please review their privacy policies before sharing information.

9. Children's Privacy

CodeBridge is not intended for individuals under the age of 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When you delete your account, we remove personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries where our servers or service providers are located. We ensure adequate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes via email or platform notifications. Continued use of CodeBridge after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
